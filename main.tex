			\end{tabu}

			\textbf{Abstract}

			\zihao{-4} This paper summarizes the research results of
			scene based nonuniformity correction of infrared focal
			plane point array. After summarizing the advantages,
			disadvantages and limitations of the classical
			algorithms, the new algorithms emerging in recent years
			are introduced. Finally, by analyzing the
			characteristics of the new algorithm, the future
			research direction and development trend of scene
			correction are predicted.
